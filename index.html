Evolution of Smart Cities
As smart cities develop, a growing network of interconnected devices known as the Internet of Things (IoT), is transforming the current landscape as we know it. These technological advances are reshaping how cities operate and how people interact in their day-to-day lives.
In the day of tomorrow, nearly every object becomes a point of data. Pedestrian crossings track foot traffic, public transport adapts to rider patterns and home devices manage and adjust to temperature, energy usage, security and human health.
Through the use of real-time data, smart cities can improve the power of technology, improve urban infrastructure and enhance public services. As technology advances, it will pave a way for a connected future and higher quality of life for citizens.
